## Leakage and compute boundaries

- Public, development, and heldout artifacts are content-addressed.
- Development contains training and calibration only; heldout is a separate
  sealed file. Their case-ID overlap is zero.
- Development calibration had no heldout-sidecar argument and reported
  `heldout_sidecar_loaded=false`.
- Heldout preflight had no heldout-sidecar argument. It bound the sealed file by
  the public separation receipt and did not inspect answer values or cardinality.
- The heldout sidecar was first opened by the evaluator after LakatoTree server
  prediction registration and local preregistration.
- All model work ran on the Dell sandbox against the frozen
  `Qwen/Qwen3.6-35B-A3B-FP8` revision with temperature 0, seed 9173,
  thinking disabled, and exact tokenizer parity.

## Claim boundary

Supported:

- training-outcome-derived typed policy;
- evidence-bound admission of that policy;
- causal frozen-model answer change versus no memory and removed control;
- generalization from one training case to six fresh heldout questions.

Not supported:

- a learned compiler advantage over raw transcripts as a general claim;
- result-to-credit-to-`ΔW` learning;
- Agent A to Agent B transfer;
- learned topology rewiring;
- long-term consolidation or sleep.

The raw transcript solved one of six heldout cases, while the typed policy solved
all six. Raw-transcript superiority was intentionally reported, not used as a
PASS gate.

## LakatoTree state

The preregistered metric improved from baseline 0 to 6, and `submit_result`
derived `lakatos=progressive`. The independent deterministic judge receipt says
`PASS`.

LakatoTree nevertheless stores the node as
`partial@L0(client_asserted,replay_refuted)`. Its server replay command assumes a
one-positional-result-path judge interface, while the frozen P1v3 judge requires
explicit `--evidence --budget --output` arguments. This is a replay-interface
contract mismatch, not a heldout metric failure. The judge must not be edited
and rerun on already-seen heldout data.

The original question `Q-p1v3-nonredundant-policy-lesson-actuation` is closed by
`p1v3-l0-policy-actuation-seed3-20260724`. The replay limitation moved to the new
question `Q-p1v4-server-replay-compatible-judge-seam`.
