## Seed-4 abort and R2 repair

The first fresh seed-4 cut was aborted before gold or heldout outcomes were
opened because the public eligibility filter yielded only three single-match
candidates, below the required ten. The failure remains preserved in
`receipts/p1v4_seed4_eligibility_abort_20260724.json`.

R2 generated seed 5 and increased only the public generation pool from 30 to
200 questions per type. Selection remained deterministic, the split remained
1 training / 3 calibration / 6 heldout, and the score threshold remained three
typed improvements. This was an availability repair based on public candidate
count, not outcome-based seed shopping. Development and heldout case overlap is
zero.

## Claim boundary

Supported:

- fresh replication of training-outcome-derived typed source-policy actuation;
- causal frozen-model answer change versus no memory and removed control;
- exact-compute four-arm comparison on six new heldout questions;
- deterministic judge replay from the explicit evidence+budget contract in the
  live LakatoTree container.

Not supported:

- learned compiler superiority over raw transcript as a general claim;
- result-to-credit-to-`ΔW` closed-loop learning;
- Agent A to Agent B learning transfer;
- learned topology rewiring;
- long-term consolidation or sleep.

The sample is still small (`n=6`), the tree assurance tier remains notebook,
and the prediction anchor reports `pred_anchor_verified=false`. The result is a
real preregistered replication receipt, not evidence for all of HSWM.

## Next programme step

The next hard scientific question is no longer replay infrastructure. It is the
three-factor plasticity loop:

`outcome error/reward -> used-bond eligibility credit -> fast candidate -> durable slow-weight promotion -> changed later action`

The next preregistration must include static-update, random-credit, shuffled
eligibility, and no-promotion controls, then test whether removing the promoted
rewrite removes the later behavioral gain. Agent A-to-B transfer and learned
topology rewiring remain later, separate questions.
